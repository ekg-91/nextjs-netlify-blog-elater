White Sands National Monument is an extraordinary place. A beautiful landscape, and on the day we visited, a dynamic sky, with rain showers in the distance. The name is famous because of the adjacent missile range, but restricted areas of the national monument also contain sites of great archaeological importance, including some of the oldest preserved human footprints in the Americas, over 20,000 years old.
